GULAB JAMUN

A classic Indian dessert that never fails to impress! Gulab Jamun is usually made in Indian homes on festive and special occasions. Gulab meaning rose and Jamun meaning berry makes for Gulab Jamun as a berry-sized ball dunken in rose flavoured sugar syrup. It can be served hot or just a stand-alone chilled dish.
